  • ♦️ National Resistance Brigades - Jenin: — A statement issued by the National Resistance Brigades, the military wing of the Democratic Front for the Liberation of Palestine - the Martyr Comrade Omar Al-Qasim Group - Jenin, Jenin Camp. O masses of our heroic people, O sons of Palestine in all places of their presence and in beloved Gaza, We speak to you today with our conviction that our national cause cannot be recovered except by force, for what is taken by force can only be returned by force. As you always know, we, with the rest of the national and Islamic action factions, are always present and never falter on resisting this occupier. Since the events of the camp and the aggression on Gaza and the West Bank, we have been resisting in the field with the rest of our heroic people, without caring about being seem or prominence in the screens of the media. But today we come out to you after we saw and the whole world saw the bloody massacres and the brutality practiced by this occupier against our people, who were left alone to resist in the field against the honor of this nation. We say to every honorable and free person: Mobilize! Mobilize! To all those who own weapons, go to the field to resist this occupier. We promise you to remain loyal to the blood of the martyrs, the wounded, and the prisoners. Glory and eternity to the heroic martyrs. Healing for the wounded. Freedom for the prisoners. Long live our valiant resistance. Al-Aqsa Flood.
